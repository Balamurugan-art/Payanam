🏛️ Official Tamil Nadu Travel Resources (2026)

Verify all information from these government-authorized sources only. Look for the .gov.in domain and the Srivilliputhur Temple tower emblem.

🥾

Official Trekking (35+ Trails)

Joint venture of TN Forest Dept & TNWEC. All forest treks must be booked here. Includes trained tribal guides (1:5 ratio), insurance & welcome kit.

40 Trails · 14 Districts ₹649 – ₹2,999 Closed mid-Feb to mid-Apr
✅ Min group size: 5 persons ✅ Age 9+ (Easy treks with guardian) ✅ Insurance included in fee 🚫 No solo treks in forest areas 🚫 No pets, speakers, fire, alcohol
trektamilnadu.com → 📞 +91-74189 94333 · 📧 [email protected]
🚗

Hill Station E-Pass (Mandatory 2026)

Managed by TN e-Governance Agency (TNeGA). Mandatory QR-coded pass for ALL tourist vehicles entering Ooty (Nilgiris) & Kodaikanal. Without this, you will be turned back at checkposts.

FREE · Instant approval Nilgiris: 6,000 weekday / 8,000 weekend Kodaikanal: 4,000 weekday / 6,000 weekend
✅ Apply via Mobile OTP login ✅ Digital QR on phone accepted ✅ Covers Coonoor, Kotagiri, Gudalur too ⚠️ Checkposts: Kallar, Burliyar, Kakkanallah 💡 Tip: Apply late night or before 8 AM to avoid server load
epass.tnega.org →
🛕

Temple Timings & Rituals

Thirukoil Portal by HR&CE Department, Govt of Tamil Nadu. Official source for temple hours, Annadhanam (free meal) timings, and online Abishekam/special entry tickets. Avoid scams — book only here.

✅ Temple Search for exact timings ✅ Online Abishekam booking ✅ Annadhanam schedule ⚠️ Only official portal for paid rituals
hrce.tn.gov.in →
🏕️

Eco-Huts & Forest Stays

Official Forest Department portal for sustainable tourism. Government-authorized eco-huts, guest houses, boating, safari & nature trails inside tiger reserves (Mudumalai, Anamalai, KMTR).

Eco-Huts Guest Houses Safari Boating Dark Sky Park (Kolli Hills)
